02

Every meeting
starts prepared.

For each meeting, DailyOS builds an executive briefing: who's in the room, what happened last time, and what to walk in ready for.

Acme Corp QBR
2:00 PM · 45 min · Renewal cycle · Acme Corp

The meeting ended twenty minutes ago. The follow-ups are already in your queue.

03

Capture without
taking notes.

After each meeting, DailyOS processes the transcript. Action items, decisions, and follow-ups get extracted and routed to your queue. Relationship context updates for next time.

04

The week, already mapped.

DailyOS maps your week as meetings land on the calendar: which days are heaviest, where you have space for deep work, which accounts need attention, and what your top priority should be. Intelligence accumulates throughout the week, not just at the start.

05

Every day makes the next one better.

DailyOS learns from every interaction. Meeting intelligence gets richer. Risk signals get more specific.

Two weeks in, the difference is obvious. The system knows your accounts, your patterns, the people you talk to. It doesn't forget what happened in last month's QBR.
