Local-First Architecture

Your files. Your machine.

Everything DailyOS knows lives as markdown and JSON on your machine. No cloud. No lock-in. Any AI tool can read your workspace.

~/Documents/DailyOS/ ├── _today/ │ ├── schedule.json │ ├── briefing.md │ └── prep-acme-qbr.md ├── Accounts/ │ └── Acme-Corp/ │ ├── README.md │ ├── dashboard.json │ ├── intelligence.json │ ├── dashboard.md │ ├── Call-Transcripts/ │ ├── Meeting-Notes/ │ └── Documents/ └── Projects/

DailyOS maintains a structured, AI-consumable knowledge base in markdown. The app is one view into that data. Claude Desktop, ChatGPT, or any future AI tool can read the same workspace. The archive is the product, not the app.
