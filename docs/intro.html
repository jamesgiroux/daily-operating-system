The Design Philosophy

Zero-guilt design.

Three rules that keep you using it.

  • 1
    Consuming, not producing. You shouldn't have to maintain your productivity tools. They should just be productive.
  • 2
    Works when you work. Tuesday and Thursday this week. Wednesday and Friday next week. The system adapts to your rhythm.
  • 3
    Everything changeable or removable. If it's not working for you, change it or remove it. No sacred cows.

The File Format

Everything is a .md file.

Plain text with simple formatting. Opens anywhere, pastes everywhere.

  • Portable: Copy/paste directly into WordPress, Google Docs, Notion, Slack
  • Future-proof: Plain text never becomes unreadable. No app lock-in.
  • AI-friendly: LLMs work better with plain text than complex formats
  • Searchable: grep, spotlight, any text search finds your content
That's all you need

# is a heading. **bold**, *italic*. - for bullets. That's 90% of what you need.
