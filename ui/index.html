Google Setup Step 2

Create OAuth credentials.

This lets Claude read your calendar and email.

4
Configure OAuth consent screen

"APIs & Services" ‚Üí "OAuth consent screen" ‚Üí Choose "External" ‚Üí Fill in app name ‚Üí Add your email as test user

5
Create credentials

"APIs & Services" ‚Üí "Credentials" ‚Üí "Create Credentials" ‚Üí "OAuth client ID" ‚Üí Choose "Desktop app"

6
Download and save

Download JSON ‚Üí Save as: .config/google/credentials.json

Then what?

Tell Claude: "Authorize my Google account" ‚Äî A browser opens, you sign in. Done!
