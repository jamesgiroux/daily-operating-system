What's New

Version 0.14.0

User Professional Context

You are now the smartest entity in DailyOS. The app knows your value proposition, your strategic priorities, your methodologies — and uses all of it to make every briefing, every signal, and every recommendation more relevant to you.

What's included

The /me page

A dedicated professional context surface where you define:

  • About Me: Your name, title, company, bio, role, and how you're measured
  • What I Deliver: Value proposition, success definition, pricing, differentiators, objections
  • My Priorities: Annual bets and quarterly focus areas that persist until you update them
  • My Playbooks: Your methodologies and approaches
  • Context: Professional knowledge that enriches enrichment (yes, really)
  • Attachments: Documents that provide deeper context

Smarter entity intelligence

Your professional context is embedded in every entity enrichment prompt. The system now understands your value angle and frames intelligence through your lens. A cost-pressure signal becomes an opportunity. An at-risk stakeholder is prioritized based on your quarterly focus.

Signal ranking by priority

Signals from accounts and projects you've linked to your annual or quarterly priorities rank higher. The system amplifies what matters to you.

Identity field migration

Your name, title, and company have moved from Settings to the /me page. Settings is now purely workspace config: domains, role preset, day start, personality.

Design system

The /me page uses the Eucalyptus accent color and the same editorial magazine aesthetic as the rest of DailyOS. It's not another Settings panel—it's a professional narrative about you.

Zero guilt

Annual and quarterly priorities never expire. Stale priorities are still useful context. Update them when you want to, not because the system nags you.
