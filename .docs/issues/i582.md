# I582 — Email-Meeting Linkage

**Version:** v1.0.1
**Area:** Frontend / Email
**Priority:** P1

## Problem

The email-meeting bridge (`signals/email_bridge.rs`) already emits `pre_meeting_context` signals — correlating emails from meeting attendees with upcoming meetings. But users never see the connection. "This email from Sarah is relevant to your 2pm with Acme" is exactly the kind of insight a chief of staff would whisper before you walk into a room. The intelligence is computed and paid for, but invisible.

## Design

### Backend

**Extend email data** in `get_emails_enriched()`: For each email, query `signal_events` for `pre_meeting_context` signals where the signal's entity or sender matches the email. Return as `meeting_links: Vec<MeetingLink>` on each `EnrichedEmail`.

**New struct** `MeetingLink`:
- `meeting_id`
- `meeting_title`
- `meeting_time` (ISO timestamp)
- `link_reason` (e.g., "sender is attending", "same account")

Only link meetings within the next 48 hours (matching existing bridge window in `email_bridge.rs`).

### Frontend — The Correspondent

Emails with meeting links show a subtle **meeting tag** in the `EmailIntelItem` meta line:
- Format: "Before: {meeting_title} at {time}" (e.g., "Before: Acme QBR at 2pm")
- Clicking the tag navigates to meeting detail page
- Tag uses existing editorial styling (no new visual pattern — treat like entity chip)
- Emails with no meeting linkage show no tag (no empty placeholder)

### Frontend — Meeting Detail

In the meeting prep/briefing section, show **"Recent correspondence"** with linked email summaries.
- Verify if `gather_email_context()` output already renders on meeting detail — if so, ensure it's visible and well-formatted
- If not rendered, add a section showing email summaries from the bridge context
- Each item: sender, subject, AI summary, date

### No New Signals

No new signal emission needed — `pre_meeting_context` is already emitted by the bridge. This issue is purely about surfacing what's already computed.

## Mock Data

Requires I596 (email mock data foundation). Meeting linkage scenarios:
- At least 2 mock emails where sender matches an attendee of a mock meeting happening today
- 1 mock `pre_meeting_context` signal linking email to upcoming meeting
- Mock meetings already exist for today — just need email sender overlap with attendees

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Emails linked to upcoming meetings (via `pre_meeting_context` signals) show a meeting tag on The Correspondent
2. Tag shows meeting title and time (e.g., "Before: Acme QBR at 2pm")
3. Clicking the tag navigates to the meeting detail page
4. Meeting detail page shows linked email summaries in the prep/briefing section
5. Only meetings within the next 48 hours are linked (matching existing bridge window)
6. Emails with no meeting linkage show no tag (no empty/placeholder state)
7. **Mock data**: At least 2 mock emails from meeting attendees seed `pre_meeting_context` signals. Meeting tag renders on those emails in `full` scenario.
