# I142 — Account Plan Artifact

**Status:** Open (Parking Lot)
**Priority:** P2
**Version:** Unscheduled
**Area:** Entity

## Summary

An "Account Plan" is a structured document that captures strategy, goals, stakeholders, risks, and action plan for a key account — the kind of document a sales rep or CS manager would maintain for a top customer. This issue covers generating and maintaining an Account Plan artifact from the intelligence already in DailyOS: entity intelligence, meeting history, actions, signals, and stakeholder profiles.

The Account Plan would be a generated artifact (like the risk briefing) that the user can edit, share, and export — the product's take on what a modern account plan looks like when driven by AI rather than manually maintained.

## Acceptance Criteria

Not yet specified. The artifact should include: account overview, health status, key stakeholders, strategic programs, open actions, recent wins/risks, and forward-looking strategy section. It should be regeneratable from the latest intelligence and editable by the user.

## Dependencies

- Depends on entity intelligence pipeline maturity.
- Related to I258 (Report Mode) and I302 (PDF export) — the Account Plan would likely be exported in those formats.
- Related to I347 (SWOT report type).

## Notes / Rationale

Parked because the intelligence quality and data completeness required to produce a trustworthy Account Plan were not there when this was logged. The entity intelligence architecture (v0.10.0+) and portfolio intelligence (v0.13.3) make this more tractable. Still not prioritized because I258, I302, and I347 address similar report export needs.
