# I633 — Health scoring real-time accuracy: scores must update post-meeting

**Area:** Backend / Health Scoring + Signals
**Priority:** P0
**Version:** v1.0.3
**Effort:** Medium
**Source:** CEO plan review (2026-03-21). User reports health scores are inaccurate and don't update post-meeting without full enrichment/refresh. This undermines confidence in the entire health scoring system.

---

## Problem

Health scores should update when new meeting data arrives (transcript processed, signals emitted). Currently, `recompute_entity_health()` is called after transcript Phase 3 completes (`transcript.rs` line 657), but the scores may not actually change because:

1. The health dimensions may depend on data that only updates during full PTY enrichment (not available from transcript signals alone)
2. Signal-driven dimension updates may not be wired — signals fire but dimension inputs may not read them
3. The `compute_account_health()` function may require intelligence data that's stale between enrichment cycles
4. Meeting frequency dimensions may compute from DB queries that don't include the just-processed meeting

The result: a CSM processes a transcript, checks the account health score, and it hasn't changed. They lose trust in the system.

## Investigation Required

Before implementing, diagnose which of these causes is active:

1. **Check `recompute_entity_health()` path.** Does it actually call `compute_account_health()`? Does it write the result to `entity_quality`? Is the write visible to the frontend query?
2. **Check dimension inputs.** Do meeting_cadence, champion_health, email_engagement dimensions read from DB tables that include the just-processed meeting data?
3. **Check signal wiring.** After transcript processing emits `transcript_outcomes` signal, does any propagation rule trigger a health rescore?
4. **Check frontend cache.** Does the account detail page re-fetch health data after a transcript is processed, or is it showing a cached value?

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Process a transcript for an account meeting. Health score updates within 60 seconds — visible on account dossier without manual refresh.
2. Meeting cadence dimension reflects the just-processed meeting (frequency count incremented, recency bonus applied).
3. Champion health dimension updates if the transcript extracted champion health data.
4. The `transcript_outcomes` signal triggers health rescore via propagation rules (if not already wired).
5. Frontend re-fetches health data after transcript processing (event-driven, not polling).
6. Account with 1 meeting gets a health score with confidence qualifier (sparse data handling).
7. Health score change appears in the next daily briefing's lifecycle intelligence (if I623 is also shipped).
