# I578 ‚Äî Render Replies Needed

**Version:** v1.0.1
**Area:** Frontend / Email
**Priority:** P1

## Problem

`EmailBriefingData` already returns `repliesNeeded: DirectiveReplyNeeded[]` from the backend ‚Äî AI-detected emails where the content indicates a reply is expected (a question was asked, a decision is pending, etc.). The frontend ignores this field entirely. This is zero-backend-work value.

## Design

### Frontend

Render `repliesNeeded` items as a sub-group within YOUR MOVE, or as a standalone section between YOUR MOVE and COMMITMENTS if YOUR MOVE is empty.

Each item shows:
- Sender name
- Subject (truncated to 40 chars)
- AI-extracted reason a reply is needed (the `reason` field from `DirectiveReplyNeeded`)
- Entity name if available

Actions:
- Dismiss (existing `dismiss_email_item` pattern, type: `"reply_needed"`)
- Open in Gmail (deep link)

### Deduplication with I577

If I577 (reply debt) lands first or concurrently: an email can appear in both "AWAITING YOUR REPLY" (structural ‚Äî age-based) and "replies needed" (content ‚Äî AI-extracted). Deduplicate by `email_id` ‚Äî show once in "AWAITING YOUR REPLY" with an annotation like "reply expected" to indicate both signals fired. If I577 is not yet implemented, render replies needed independently.

## Mock Data

Requires I596 (email mock data foundation). `repliesNeeded` is computed by the backend enrichment pipeline from email content ‚Äî mock emails with `enrichment_state = 'enriched'` and question-containing summaries will naturally produce `repliesNeeded` entries. Verify at least 2 items appear in mock data.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `repliesNeeded` items from `EmailBriefingData` render on The Correspondent page
2. Each item shows sender, subject, and the AI-extracted reason a reply is needed
3. Items that also appear in reply debt (I577) are deduplicated ‚Äî shown once with both signals
4. Dismissing a reply-needed item emits a dismissal signal (existing `dismiss_email_item` pattern)
5. Items not rendered when `repliesNeeded` is empty (no empty section)
6. **Mock data**: At least 2 `repliesNeeded` items render from `full` scenario mock emails
