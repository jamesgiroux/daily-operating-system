# I598 — Reactive Health Recomputation on Signal Arrival

**Version:** v1.0.0 Phase 5b
**Depends on:** I499 (health scoring engine), I555 (behavioral dimension upgrade)
**Type:** Bug fix — Backend / Intelligence Loop / Health Scoring
**Scope:** Backend: services/intelligence.rs, intelligence/health_scoring.rs, glean_provider.rs, processor/transcript.rs, signals/propagation.rs

---

## Problem

Health is computed ONCE during enrichment and never recomputed when new signals arrive. The Intelligence Loop diagram shows `Signals → Health Scoring` but this arrow doesn't actually fire.

Three dimensions are affected:

1. **Champion health** — reads `meeting_champion_health` table (I555 behavioral data). When a new meeting transcript is processed and champion health rows are inserted, the health dimension stays stale until the next full enrichment cycle (2+ hours).

2. **Stakeholder coverage** — reads `account_stakeholders` table + meeting recency. When stakeholder roles are synced or new meetings add attendance data, the dimension is stale.

3. **Email engagement** — reads `email_signals` table. When new email signals arrive for an account, the engagement dimension doesn't update.

The 2-hour enrichment TTL means health dimensions can be hours behind the actual state of the account.

### Evidence

- `intel_queue.rs:813-827` — health computed once at enrichment time
- `signals/propagation.rs:141-177` — 11 propagation rules registered, NONE trigger health recompute
- `health_scoring.rs:417` — `champion_health` reads `meeting_champion_health` (written by transcript processor, not re-read after)
- `health_scoring.rs:300` — `email_engagement` reads `email_signals` (written by email poller, not re-read after)
- `health_scoring.rs:339` — `stakeholder_coverage` reads `account_stakeholders` (written by role sync, not re-read after)

---

## Solution

### 1. Extract `recompute_entity_health()` service function

In `services/intelligence.rs`:

```rust
pub fn recompute_entity_health(
    db: &ActionDb,
    entity_id: &str,
    entity_type: &str,
) -> Result<(), String>
```

This function:
1. Returns early if `entity_type != "account"` (health is account-only for now)
2. Reads existing intelligence from DB
3. Calls `compute_account_health(db, entity_id)` to recompute all 6 dimensions
4. Updates the `health` field on the existing intelligence
5. Writes back to DB via `upsert_entity_intelligence`
6. Writes to disk for MCP sidecar (best-effort)

Must NOT trigger full re-enrichment. Must NOT call PTY or Glean. Pure algorithmic recomputation from DB data. Completes in < 100ms.

### 2. Call from signal paths

| Trigger | File | When |
|---------|------|------|
| Glean signals emitted | `glean_provider.rs` `emit_glean_signals()` | After CRM/Gong/Zendesk signals written |
| Meeting transcript processed | `processor/transcript.rs` | After Phase 2 (champion health + dynamics written) |
| Stakeholder roles synced | `services/intelligence.rs` `update_stakeholders()` | After account_stakeholders updated |
| Email signals created | `services/emails.rs` or `prepare/email_enrich.rs` | After email_signals inserted for an account |

Each call site: wrap in `if entity_type == "account"`, log-and-continue on failure.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. After a meeting transcript is processed with champion health data, the account's health score updates within 5 seconds (not waiting for next enrichment cycle).
2. After stakeholder roles are edited and synced, the stakeholder_coverage dimension updates immediately.
3. After new email signals arrive for an account, the email_engagement dimension updates within the email polling cycle.
4. After Glean signals are emitted (CRM, Gong, Zendesk), health dimensions that consume those signals update immediately.
5. Health recompute is algorithmic only — no PTY/Glean calls, no AI, completes in < 100ms.
6. Health recompute failures are logged but never block the triggering operation.
7. The Intelligence Loop's `Signals → Health Scoring` arrow is real, not aspirational.
