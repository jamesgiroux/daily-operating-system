State of play

What the account feels like right now

Acme is our strongest enterprise account because it combines three things that rarely coexist for long: executive sponsorship, delivered value, and a believable next phase. Sarah Chen's sponsorship is real, the first phase overperformed, and there is still room to grow. That is the upward story.

The downward story is more concentrated. NPS softness inside engineering is beginning to matter. Legal is moving too slowly on the amendment. Most importantly, the technical backbone of the first phase is walking out the door before the next one is fully framed. The account is not under threat. It is under pressure.

The page should preserve this tension instead of flattening it into a tidy green state.
