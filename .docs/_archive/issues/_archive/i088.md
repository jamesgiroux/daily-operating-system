# I088 — Monthly Book Intelligence — Portfolio Report

**Status:** Open (Parking Lot)
**Priority:** P2
**Version:** Unscheduled
**Area:** Intelligence

## Summary

A monthly automated report that summarizes the health and trajectory of the user's entire account portfolio. Analogous to a "monthly business review" that a chief of staff would prepare — covering which accounts improved, which declined, what patterns emerged across the book, and what to prioritize heading into the next month. This is a higher-level synthesis than the daily briefing or weekly forecast, produced on a monthly cadence from accumulated intelligence and signal history.

## Acceptance Criteria

Not yet specified. Intended to build on the entity intelligence infrastructure and portfolio intelligence work in v0.13.3 (I384, I393).

## Dependencies

- Likely depends on I384 (parent account portfolio intelligence) for the synthesis pattern.
- Related to I258 (Report Mode) and I302 (PDF export).

## Notes / Rationale

Parked because the entity intelligence pipeline (the data source this report would draw from) was not mature enough at the time this was logged. Now that v0.13.3 introduces portfolio-level intelligence for parent accounts, this becomes more tractable. Still not version-locked due to competing priorities.
