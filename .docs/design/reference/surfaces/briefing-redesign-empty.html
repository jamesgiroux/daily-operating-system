Daily Briefing

Your day, when DailyOS can read it.

The briefing is a synthesis of meetings, signals, and changes from the last 24 hours. To shape one, DailyOS needs a calendar to read.

  • ○Connect Google to bring in your calendar and email signals.
  • ○Optional: Glean for cross-tool signals (Gong, Zendesk, Slack, Linear).
  • ○Optional: Claude Code to enable AI prep on each meeting.
