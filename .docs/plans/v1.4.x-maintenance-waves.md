## What this is

A wave plan for the ~130 open issues in the Codebase Maintenance & Production Quality Linear project. Filed in isolation over months against a moving substrate; this plan organizes them by theme, dependency, and architectural relationship to v1.4.x.

This plan **explicitly applies an architectural lens** to every ticket via codex L0 prep:

- **SUBSUMED** — v1.4.x work in flight or recently shipped resolves the issue as a side-effect; the maintenance ticket should fold into the v1.4.x ticket and close.
- **BLOCKED** — fix depends on v1.4.x substrate not yet landed (signal-policy registry W1-B, durable invalidation W1-C, ability-runtime W0.5, source taxonomy W1-A, etc.); cannot ship until that substrate closes.
- **CONFLICTS** — proposed fix would revert/break v1.4.0+ ADRs (re-introduce raw `with_db` patterns ServiceContext deprecates, add tx semantics that fight claim-substrate atomicity, create duplicate enums where ADR-0107 already provides one, etc.); needs design pivot.
- **ORTHOGONAL** — ship now, no architectural exposure.
