# v0.13.6 Version Brief

**Thesis:** Maximum commands.rs extraction. v0.13.2 extracted four core services but `commands.rs` still held 8,389 lines with ~49 thick handlers. Target: reduce commands.rs by 1,400+ lines. One UX fix (I401) rounds out the version.

## Acceptance Criteria

### I401 — Show internal attendees for internal meetings

1. Open the meeting detail page for "Enterprise Team - Weekly Sync" (or any internal team meeting). "The Room" shows the accepted attendees — not empty.
2. Open the meeting detail page for "Jane <> VIP" (or any external customer meeting). "The Room" shows only external attendees — internal colleagues are still filtered.
3. The determination uses `meeting.meeting_type` (team_sync, internal, one_on_one), not a heuristic on attendee email domains.

---

### I402 — IntelligenceService extraction

1. `services/intelligence.rs` exists with public methods per the IntelligenceService contract in SERVICE-CONTRACTS.md:
   - `get_executive()` — executive intelligence assembly
   - `enrich_account(id)`, `enrich_person(id)`, `enrich_project(id)` — queue AI enrichment
   - `update_field(entity_id, field, value)` — user edits with signal emission
   - `update_stakeholders(entity_id, stakeholders)` — stakeholder edits with signal emission
   - `create_person_from_stakeholder(stakeholder)` — person creation from stakeholder card
   - `get_hygiene_status()` — hygiene scan results
   - `generate_risk_briefing()`, `get_risk_briefing()`, `save_risk_briefing()` — risk briefing lifecycle
2. All intelligence command handlers in `commands.rs` are thin wrappers.
3. Signal emissions use `emit_signal_and_propagate()`.
4. `commands.rs` line count further reduced. Measure before and after.
5. `cargo test` passes. `cargo clippy -- -D warnings` passes. IPC surface unchanged — no frontend changes.

---

### I403 — SignalService formalization

1. A SignalService API exists (either `services/signals.rs` or `signals/service.rs`) with methods per the SignalService contract in SERVICE-CONTRACTS.md:
   - `emit(db, state, entity_type, entity_id, signal_type, source, value, confidence)` — wraps `emit_signal_and_propagate` with engine from AppState
   - `get_for_entity(entity_id)`
   - `get_callouts(entity_id)`
   - `invalidate_preps(entity_id)`
2. All service methods in actions.rs, accounts.rs, people.rs, meetings.rs, and the new intelligence.rs call SignalService instead of `bus::emit_signal_and_propagate()` directly.
3. Service methods no longer need `engine: &PropagationEngine` or `&AppState` passed solely for signal emission — the SignalService handles engine access internally.
4. `cargo test` passes. `cargo clippy -- -D warnings` passes. IPC surface unchanged.
