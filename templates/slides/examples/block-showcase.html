Risk Register
APAC Onboarding Backlog
14 accounts are waiting for onboarding resources in the APAC region. Current capacity supports 4 onboardings per month. Without additional headcount or process changes, the backlog won't clear until Q3. Recommendation: redeploy two onboarding specialists from EMEA where the queue is empty.
Dashboard Latency
Real-time dashboard response times are averaging 4.2 seconds, above the 3-second target for enterprise accounts. Engineering has identified the bottleneck in the aggregation layer. Fix is in staging, expected to ship in Sprint 14.
