The Partnership
Pinnacle has been on the platform for three years. In that time, you've grown from a single marketing site to a multi-property digital ecosystem serving four business lines and three regulatory jurisdictions.
This year's focus has been consolidation: bringing the APAC properties onto the same infrastructure, standardizing the content governance model, and preparing the platform for the AI capabilities your innovation team has been evaluating.
Where We Stand
What's Working
Platform uptime: 99.97% over trailing 12 months
APAC migration: 3 of 4 properties consolidated
Content governance framework adopted across all business lines
Developer velocity up — deploy frequency doubled since Q2 2025
What Needs Attention
4th APAC property (Japan) blocked on data residency review
Performance optimization needed for the wealth management portal
AI readiness assessment not yet started — was a Q1 goal
Value Delivered
APAC consolidation reduced Pinnacle's infrastructure footprint from 6 vendors to 2, saving an estimated $340K annually in licensing and operational overhead.
$340K annual savings

Developer deploy frequency doubled after migrating to the standardized CI/CD pipeline. The team went from bi-weekly releases to multiple deploys per day with no increase in incidents.
2x deploy frequency, zero incident increase
